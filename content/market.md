The 561 Group Market is a catalog of independent enterprises, not a platform
that takes custody of them. Every offer identifies the enterprise that owns
the boundary, public source, operational endpoint, price, chain/payee policy,
and evidence through which a customer can check the claim.

## Paid Ephemeris Gateway — live on Base

**Owner:** RSC Quiescent / paid-ephemeris-gateway  
**Health:** [live status](https://ephemeris.561.group/v1/status)  
**Purchase route:** `GET https://ephemeris.561.group/v1/ephemeris/moment/:date`  
**Settlement:** x402 v2 exact on Base (`eip155:8453`) in USDC  
**Moment Essentials:** $0.04 USDC  
**Precision Moment:** $0.32 USDC with observer location.

The endpoint's own `402 Payment Required` challenge is authoritative for its
current asset, payee, and price. The catalog requalifies that public challenge
every six hours without attempting payment or settlement.

## Open x402 boundary source

The Apache-2.0
[x402 Boundary Reference](https://github.com/561-group/x402-boundary-reference)
is the paired public implementation for studying, running, and independently
checking an explicit x402 v2 boundary. Customers may use the source freely or
buy the separately operated Ephemeris service when they value its operation,
measurement provenance, and evidence trail.

## Sponsorship

Sponsorship funds public mathematics, protocol references, documentation,
assays, verification, and resilient operations. It does not purchase hidden
access, custody, or control of research conclusions. The current public route
accepts Base USDC on `eip155:8453` at
`0x42F72C0a340A4A55C082Fe42483e87691D2bff64`. A sponsorship transfer is not a
payment for an Ephemeris response or a claim on customer receipts.

## Listing rule

Every paid listing must be independently checkable against its public HTTPS
x402 challenge and signed provider identity. The market publishes the offer;
the owning enterprise settles and records the transaction.
